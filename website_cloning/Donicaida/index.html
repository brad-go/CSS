AboutDonica Ida is a Hawaii-born designer who loves beautiful typography and a well-told story. She is the former Creative Director of HuffPost Highline and Design Director of Mary Review. Donica lives somewhere between Brooklyn and the PNW, and splits her spare time between road trips, hikes and ramen.
